# Tahini Roasted Cabbage and Carrots
A flavorful, plant-based meal that your friends and family are sure to love!

## Ingredients 
*1 small head of cabbage, cored and thinly sliced 
*2 large carrots, peeled and thinly sliced
*2 tablespoons tahini
*2 tablespoons olive oil
*1/2 teaspoon sea salt
*1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
*1/2 cup white balsamic vinegar
*1/2 cup white sugar
*1/2 teaspoon sea salt 
*2 small red onions, thinly sliced
*1 can butter beans, rinsed and drained 
*2 tablespoons olive oil
*1/2 teaspoon sea salt

## Instructions 
1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
2. In a large bowl, combine the cabbage, carrots, tahini, olive oil, salt and pepper. Mix until all ingredients are evenly combined. 
3. Spread the mixture evenly onto a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes, stirring once halfway through. 
4. In a small pot, combine the balsamic vinegar, sugar, and salt and bring to a boil. 
5. Add the onions and cook for 1 minute. 
6. Remove from heat and transfer to a shallow bowl. Allow to cool completely. 
7. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add the butter beans. 
8. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring often, until the beans are golden and crisp.
9. Add the olive oil and salt, stirring to combine.
